About This Directory

This is a curated community bulletin board featuring private instructors, tutors, and educational programs we've connected with and believe are worth exploring. These are independent community partners, not Lifespace employees - you'll work with them directly at their own rates and terms.

Every family's needs are unique, and we encourage you to have conversations with any provider to determine if they're the right match for your situation. LifeSpace training is available to partners who request it.
