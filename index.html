Educational design services that put learners at the center. We work with homeschooling families, microschool founders, and small school teams to create learning experiences tailored to real students in real contexts.

About Lifespace Education

Lifespace Education is a small educational design practice working with homeschools, microschools, and early-stage schools. We start from the actual learners in front of you‚Äînot generic templates or off-the-shelf solutions.

We design for real schedules, families, and constraints. Our work blends print materials, conversation, and digital tools as needed to support learning that feels human-scale and manageable.

We work with families, founders, and educators who are oriented toward equity and humane learning environments, and who want their educational design to reflect those values in practice.
