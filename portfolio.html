Vada Socratic AI Tutor System

The Need: Students need help understanding their assignments, but teachers can't be available 24/7. Direct answers don't build learning.

The Solution: Vada is a Socratic AI tutor that guides students to discover answers themselves through questions. Teachers upload any assignment (PDF or text), and Vada becomes an expert in that content—asking questions, pointing to relevant sections, helping students think critically without giving direct answers. Teacher dashboard includes AI workbook generator that creates formatted lessons with comprehension and reflection questions at specified content and reading levels.

US History Interactive Textbook - Enslaved Africans in America

The Need: History textbooks often center white perspectives. Students need content that centers marginalized experiences with built-in Socratic support.

The Solution: Interactive web textbook with embedded Vada AI tutor. Students read chapter content centered on enslaved Africans' agency and resistance, then engage with Vada for Socratic dialogue about the material. AI guides students to deeper understanding through questions, never giving direct answers. Mobile-accessible, saves progress.

Ahupuaʻa Learning Journey (AI-Enhanced)

Students explore ahupuaʻa systems (traditional Hawaiian land divisions), Indigenous ecological knowledge, colonial disruption, and sovereignty movements. About Our Design Practice

Lifespace Education is a small educational design practice working with homeschools, microschools, and early-stage schools. We start from the actual learners in front of you—not generic templates or off-the-shelf solutions.

We design for real schedules, families, and constraints. Our work blends print materials, conversation, and digital tools as needed to support learning that feels human-scale and manageable.

We work with families, founders, and educators who are oriented toward equity and humane learning environments, and who want their educational design to reflect those values in practice.
