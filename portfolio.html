Educational Design Services

Custom learning plans, curriculum development, instruction, and training for homeschools, microschools, and small schools.

We help you shape learning directly with your students, without institutional gatekeepers in the way.

What We Offer

Custom Learning Plans

Individual instructional profiles coordinating goals, rhythms, subjects, and resources for specific learners in homeschools, microschools, or small schools.

Custom Curriculum & Materials

Complete curriculum design, workbooks, printables, and leveled reading materials built for your specific context and learners. Typical turnaround: under 24 hours.

AI-Powered Learning Tools

Interactive curriculum with embedded Socratic AI tutors that guide students to discover answers through questions - 24/7 support that builds understanding.

Custom Classes & Instruction

Tailored class design and direct instruction for children, teens, or adult learners - including training for founders and educators.

About Our Design Practice

Lifespace Education is a small educational design practice working with homeschools, microschools, and early-stage schools. We start from the actual learners in front of you - not generic templates or off-the-shelf solutions.

We design for real schedules, families, and constraints. Our work blends print materials, conversation, and digital tools as needed to support learning that feels human-scale and manageable.

We work with families, founders, and educators who are oriented toward equity and humane learning environments, and who want their educational design to reflect those values in practice.
